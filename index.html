About D.J.

I am a native Delawarean. I currently live in Frederica with my wife Tiffanie, daughter Callie (5) and son Spencer (2). I am a graduate from Lake Forest high school. After high school I attended Delaware Tech community college where I received an associate's degree in Criminal Justice. I was hired to be a police officer for Delaware State University. I attended the Delaware State Police Academy. I was severely injured as a recruit. The long term effects of the injury prevented my graduation from the academy. Once recovered I enrolled at Delaware Tech community college in a commercial drivers course. Upon completion I received my Class A Commercial drivers license. I currently drive a tractor trailer hauling liquid propane.
